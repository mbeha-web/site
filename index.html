About the Firm

Your Legal Partner in Botswana

Mbeha Attorneys is a full-service law firm based in Kasane, Botswana, providing expert legal counsel across a broad spectrum of practice areas. As licensed Attorneys, Notaries, and Conveyancers, we serve individuals, families, and businesses with the highest standard of professionalism.

We understand that legal challenges can be stressful and complex. Our team brings clarity, strategy, and compassion to every matter — so you can focus on what matters most while we handle the rest.

Practice Areas

Our Areas of Law

Comprehensive legal representation tailored to your needs, delivered by experienced professionals who know Botswana law inside out.

Bankruptcy or Liquidation

We protect you from aggressive debt collectors and creditors. You don't have to lose your job or face civil imprisonment — we offer tailored solutions.

Land Disputes

Expert resolution of land and property conflicts, title transfers, boundary disputes, and unlawful evictions under Botswana's property law framework.

Civil Disputes

Strategic representation in civil claims including breach of contract, damages, debt recovery, and injunctions before the Magistrate and High Courts.

General Criminal Litigation

Robust criminal defence from arrest through trial. We safeguard your constitutional rights and fight for the best possible outcome in every case.

Road Traffic Offences

Defending clients against traffic charges including reckless driving, DUI, licence suspensions, and accident-related claims under Botswana traffic law.

Conveyancing & Notary

Professional property transfers, deed registration, notarial services, antenuptial contracts, and all conveyancing matters handled with precision.

Business & Commercial

Corporate structuring, contract drafting, regulatory compliance, and commercial dispute resolution to protect and grow your business interests.

Family & Domestic

Compassionate guidance through divorce, child custody, maintenance, domestic violence protection orders, and all family law matters in Botswana.
